我们深知隐私对您的重要性，并致力于保护您的个人信息。本隐私政策说明了我们如何收集、使用、存储、共享和保护您的信息。

重要提示：我们不收集任何个人身份信息（PII）。您的情绪记录仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至我们的服务器。

一、我们如何收集和使用您的信息

1.1 您直接提供的信息

  • 情绪记录：当您使用应用记录情绪时，相关数据（如情绪类型、应对方法、备注）仅保存在您的设备本地
  • 订阅信息：通过Apple进行订阅处理，我们会接收订阅状态以解锁Pro功能

1.2 自动收集的信息

我们不会收集以下信息：

  • 位置信息
  • 设备标识符
  • 使用数据
  • Cookies

1.3 Apple处理的信息

订阅购买由Apple处理。我们仅通过Apple的服务器验证订阅状态，不会获取您的支付信息、信用卡号或银行账户信息。

二、我们如何使用信息

由于我们不收集个人信息，我们不会将您的任何信息用于：

  • 广告推送
  • 数据分析
  • 出售给第三方
  • 营销目的

三、信息存储与保护

3.1 本地存储

您的所有数据均存储在您的个人设备上：

  • 情绪记录 - 仅本地存储
  • 订阅状态 - 仅本地缓存
  • 应用设置 - 仅本地存储

3.2 数据安全

安全说明：由于数据仅存储在您的设备上，数据安全性由您设备的系统安全机制保护。我们建议您启用设备密码保护以确保安全。

四、第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

服务 用途 数据处理
RevenueCat 订阅管理 仅验证订阅状态，不收集用户数据
Apple (IAP) 应用内购买 Apple处理，不共享支付信息

五、您的权利

根据适用法律，您享有以下权利：

  • 访问权：您可以查看存储在设备上的所有数据
  • 删除权：您可以随时删除应用及所有本地数据
  • 更正权：您可以编辑或修改您的情绪记录

如需删除所有数据，只需卸载应用即可。卸载后，所有本地存储的数据将被永久删除。

六、儿童隐私

年龄要求：本应用不面向13岁以下（或当地法律规定的最低年龄）的儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

七、政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更时，我们将：

  • 在应用内通知您
  • 更新页面顶部的"生效日期"

八、联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系我们：

邮箱：govomusic@gmail.com

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

We understand the importance of privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information.

Important: We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). Your mood records are stored locally on your device only and are not uploaded to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Mood Records: When you record your mood, data (such as mood type, coping method, notes) is stored locally on your device only
  • Subscription Info: Through Apple subscription processing, we receive subscription status to unlock Pro features

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect:

  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data
  • Cookies

1.3 Apple-Processed Information

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We only verify subscription status through Apple's servers and do not receive your payment information, credit card numbers, or bank account details.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your information for:

  • Advertising
  • Data analysis
  • Selling to third parties
  • Marketing purposes

3. Data Storage & Protection

3.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored on your personal device:

  • Mood Records - Local only
  • Subscription Status - Local cache only
  • App Settings - Local only

3.2 Data Security

Security Note: Since data is stored only on your device, data security is protected by your device's system security mechanisms. We recommend enabling device password protection to ensure safety.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Processing
RevenueCat Subscription Management Verifies subscription status only, no user data collected
Apple (IAP) In-App Purchases Processed by Apple, no payment info shared

5. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can view all data stored on your device
  • Deletion: You can delete the app and all local data at any time
  • Correction: You can edit or modify your mood records

To delete all data, simply uninstall the app. Upon uninstallation, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: This app is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will:

  • Notify you within the app
  • Update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

Email: govomusic@gmail.com
