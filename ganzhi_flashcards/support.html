FAQ

How to start learning?

Open the app and choose "Tiangan Learning" or "Dizhi Learning" from the home page. The app uses flashcards, and we recommend learning step by step in order.

What are Tiangan and Dizhi?

Tiangan and Dizhi are ancient Chinese symbols used for timekeeping. The Ten Tiangan are: Jia, Yi, Bing, Ding, Wu, Ji, Geng, Xin, Ren, Gui. The Twelve Dizhi are: Zi, Chou, Yin, Mao, Chen, Si, Wu, Wei, Shen, You, Xu, Hai.

Does the app require internet?

No. TianganDizhi Cards is a fully offline app. All features work without internet connection. All learning content is built into the app.

How to test learning progress?

The app provides multiple test modes: sequential test, random test, matching test, and Five Elements test. You can choose the appropriate test based on your learning progress.

Does the app save my learning progress?

No. The app is a fully local app and does not save any user data. Every time you open the app, it's a fresh learning experience.

What age group is it suitable for?

The app is suitable for all ages. The content is simple and easy to understand, perfect for students, traditional culture enthusiasts, and users who want to learn the basics of Bazi.

Learning Tips

This is an educational app designed to help users learn traditional Chinese culture. We recommend learning 5-10 cards daily for the best results.
