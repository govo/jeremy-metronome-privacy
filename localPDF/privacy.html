LocalPDF - Offline PDF Toolkit

LocalPDF - 离线 PDF 工具箱

Data Collection and Processing

数据收集与处理

LocalPDF is designed with privacy as our core principle. All PDF processing operations are performed entirely on your local device. Your files are never uploaded to any server or third-party service.

LocalPDF 以隐私为核心设计原则。所有 PDF 处理操作均在您的本地设备上完成。您的文件绝不会上传至任何服务器或第三方服务。

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data or documents. Your files remain on your device throughout the entire processing workflow.

我们不会收集、存储、传输或分享您的任何个人数据或文档。您的文件在整个处理过程中都保留在您的设备上。

100% Offline: All processing happens locally on your device. Your files never leave your hands.

100% 离线处理：所有操作均在您的设备本地完成，文件绝不离开您的手中。

Local Processing Technology

本地处理技术

LocalPDF uses WebAssembly (WASM) technology to perform PDF operations entirely within your device. This includes:

LocalPDF 使用 WebAssembly (WASM) 技术在您的设备内部完成所有 PDF 操作，包括：

  • PDF merging, splitting, and page extraction
  • PDF 合并、拆分和页面提取
  • Format conversion (PDF to images, images to PDF)
  • 格式转换（PDF 转图片、图片转 PDF）
  • Compression, rotation, and grayscale conversion
  • 压缩、旋转和灰度转换
  • Watermarking, page numbering, and encryption
  • 水印、页码和加密
  • Metadata editing and document flattening
  • 元数据编辑和文档扁平化
