```
Drummy 是为鼓手与节奏制作人打造的 AI 鼓机与节奏练习工具：内置 24 套专业鼓组、16/32 步 Step Sequencer、完整的混音器和拍号体系，把"听歌拆鼓—做鼓—练鼓"装进同一台设备。

— 主要功能 —

• 24 套专业鼓组
覆盖原声、经典、复古、模拟合成、电子、Techno、Hip-hop、Urban、Funk、BreakBeat、Deep House、Trap、Crunk、纯打击乐等风格；每套鼓组自带 LEVEL / PAN / FILTER / TUNE / DECAY 控件。

• Step Sequencer 鼓机
16 / 32 步网格，lookahead 精准调度，无漂移；内置 4/4、3/4、6/8 与三连音、布鲁斯 Shuffle 等拍号语义；支持 16 步一屏显示，适配 iPhone 竖屏。

• AI 鼓识别 / 分轨
本地 LarsNet 模型，一键从任意歌曲中分离 Kick、Snare、Hi-hat、Cymbals，并自动识别 BPM 与节拍位置；支持麦克风录制、文件导入与 ReplayKit 录屏音源。

• 一键生成鼓序列
把分轨结果转成可编辑的 Pattern：在鼓机里直接拖拽微调、复制小节、套用 Groove 模板，无需从头打点。

• 变速练习
AB Loop + 多速度段、跳到下一段、预备拍；为鼓手跟练复杂段落、慢速拆解、单点强化而设计。

• Humanize 人性化
同时随机化时机与力度，让量化过度的 Pattern 立刻获得自然律动；最大偏移参考 Logic Pro MIDI Transform Humanize 设计。

• 完整混音器
实时 Compressor、Phaser、Master 链与全局 Wet；可保存到 PRESET，随时切换工程。

• 录制与导出
iOS 麦克风 / ReplayKit 系统音频 / macOS 系统音频三种来源，录完直接拖入鼓识别流程。

• 会话自动恢复
PRESET、BPM、拍号、Subdivision、Groove、混音器设置自动持久化，下次打开立刻回到工作状态。

• 跨设备一致
iPhone、iPad、Mac（Mac Catalyst）共享同一份工程，桌面端调试、移动端练习。
```

### Description（≤ 4000 chars）

```
Drummy is an AI-powered drum machine and practice tool built for drummers and beat makers. 24 pro drum kits, 16/32-step sequencer, full mixer, and a complete musical time-signature system, all in one app: listen, split, make, and practice drums on a single device.

— KEY FEATURES —

• 24 Pro Drum Kits
Acoustic, Classic, Vintage, Analog, Electro, Techno DM, Hip-hop, Urban, Funk, BreakBeat, Deep House, Trap DM, Crunk, and Percussion. Every kit ships with per-voice LEVEL, PAN, FILTER, TUNE, and DECAY controls.

• Step Sequencer
16 / 32-step grid, lookahead scheduling for sample-accurate timing, with first-class support for 4/4, 3/4, 6/8, triplets, and blues shuffle. 16 steps fit on a single iPhone screen in portrait.

• AI Drum Recognition & Stem Separation
On-device LarsNet model splits any song into Kick, Snare, Hi-hat, and Cymbals, while detecting BPM and onsets. Capture audio from the microphone, imported files, or ReplayKit (iOS) / system audio (macOS).

• One-Tap Pattern Generation
Turn recognition results into an editable Pattern. Drag notes, duplicate bars, or apply groove templates, all inside the drum machine. No need to start from an empty grid.

• Tempo Drill
AB loop, multiple speed segments, skip-to-next, optional count-in. Built for drummers practicing tough passages, learning new parts, or drilling kick placement.

• Humanize
Randomize both timing and velocity in one move, modeled after Logic Pro's MIDI Transform Humanize. Bring quantized patterns back to life with a single tap.

• Full Mixer
Real-time compressor, phaser, and master chain. Save your full signal flow to a PRESET and recall it instantly.

• Recording
Microphone (iOS / macOS), ReplayKit on iOS, and ScreenCaptureKit on macOS. Recorded audio feeds straight into the recognition flow.

• Session Persistence
Active PRESET, BPM, time signature, subdivision, groove, and mixer settings are saved automatically. Reopen the app right where you left off.

• Universal App
iPhone, iPad, and Mac (Mac Catalyst) share the same project file, so you can sketch on the move and refine at the desk.
```
