Privacy Policy

Data Collection

CatMeows does not collect any personal user data.

All features of this app run locally. None of your data is uploaded or stored on our servers.

Device Permissions

This app requires the following device permissions:

  • Camera: For taking photos of your cat
  • Photo Library: For saving cat photos to your device
  • Microphone: For audio features (if recording is needed)

All permissions are only requested when you actively use them. You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

We do not share your information with any third parties.

Data Security

Since this app does not collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches.

Your photos and any personal information are stored on your local device only.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages.

Since no personal information is collected, it is especially suitable for children under parental guidance.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time.

Any significant changes will be notified within the app.
