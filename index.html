Introduction

Welcome to Jeremy Metronome (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "the App"). We highly value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information Collection and Use

Our app collects almost no information. Except for anonymous crash logs used to improve app quality, all data is stored and used locally on your device only. This includes:

  • User Settings Data: Including your app preferences such as theme settings, volume settings, metronome configurations, etc. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Song List Data: Information about song lists you create, including song names, BPM, notes, etc. This data is stored only locally on your device.
  • Recording Data: Audio recordings you create in the app. These files are stored only in your device's local storage.
  • Purchase Records: Transaction information related to in-app purchases, which is processed and stored by Apple's payment system.
  • Background Images: If you choose to customize theme backgrounds, related images are saved locally on your device.
  • Crash Logs: Error and crash information during app operation, used for improving app quality. This information does not contain any personally identifiable data.

Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to the cloud or share it with third parties. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data security.

Required Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Audio Playback: For playing metronome sounds
  • Storage Permission: For saving recordings and background images
  • Photo Library Access: Only required when you choose to customize background images
  • Network Access: For processing in-app purchases
  • Vibration: For providing haptic feedback (optional feature)

Data Deletion

You can delete your data through the following methods:

  • Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data
  • Delete specific data (such as songs, recordings, etc.) within the respective features in the app
  • Reset the app through system settings to clear all settings

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users of all ages. For users under 13 years of age, please use the App under parental guidance. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other appropriate means.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: govomusic@gmail.com

引言

欢迎使用 Jeremy Metronome（以下简称"我们"、"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私保护，并致力于保护您的个人信息安全。本隐私政策旨在帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

信息收集与使用

我们的应用几乎不收集任何信息。除了用于改进应用质量的匿名崩溃日志外，所有数据都只在您的设备本地存储和使用。具体包括以下内容：

  • 用户设置数据：包括您的应用偏好设置，如主题设置、音量设置、节拍器配置等。这些数据存储在您的设备本地。
  • 歌曲列表数据：您创建的歌曲列表信息，包括歌曲名称、BPM、备注等。这些数据仅保存在您的设备本地。
  • 录音数据：您在应用中创建的录音文件。这些文件仅保存在您的设备本地存储中。
  • 购买记录：与应用内购买相关的交易信息，这些信息由 Apple 的支付系统处理和存储。
  • 背景图片：如果您选择自定义主题背景，相关图片将保存在您的设备本地。
  • 崩溃日志：应用运行时的错误和崩溃信息，用于改进应用质量。这些信息不包含任何可识别个人身份的数据。

数据存储和安全

所有用户数据均存储在您的设备本地，我们不会将这些数据上传到云端或与第三方共享。我们采取适当的技术措施来保护您的数据安全。

必需的权限说明

本应用需要以下权限才能正常运行：

  • 音频播放：用于播放节拍器声音
  • 存储权限：用于保存录音文件和背景图片
  • 相册访问：仅当您选择自定义背景图片时需要
  • 网络访问：用于处理应用内购买
  • 震动：用于提供触觉反馈（可选功能）

数据删除

您可以通过以下方式删除您的数据：

  • 卸载应用将删除所有本地存储的数据
  • 在应用内的相应功能中删除特定数据（如歌曲、录音等）
  • 通过系统设置重置应用将清除所有设置

儿童隐私

我们的服务面向所有年龄段用户。对于未满 13 岁的用户，请在监护人指导下使用本应用。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。当我们进行重大更改时，我们会通过应用内通知或其他适当方式通知您。
