引言

English Version

Introduction

Welcome to Jeremy Metronome. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information Collection

Our application does not collect any personal information. All settings (such as tempo, beats, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

The app only stores the following information locally:

  • Metronome tempo settings
  • Metronome volume settings
  • Interface theme preferences

Data Usage

Stored settings are only used to remember your preferences for a better user experience. This data is stored entirely on your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties because we do not collect any information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the privacy policy, please contact: govomusic@gmail.com
